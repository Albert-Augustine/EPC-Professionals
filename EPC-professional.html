Welcome to EPC Professionals

EPC Professionals is your one-stop resource for job researching tools that will help assist you in landing your

next career move within the EPC and Oil & Gas industries worldwide

What is EPC?

EPC stands for engineer, procure, construct. Such as the contractor typically does the whole project from start to finish.

What types of projects do EPC companies do?

Landmark projects such as; Bridges, dams, defense & nuclear, mining, oil & gas, petrochemical, pipelines, power generation, railroads, refineries, skyscrapers, telecommunications, tunnels and water…. to name a few!

How long do these types of projects last?

Depending on what part of the project timeline you enter… Typical projects can run from a few months to years! But don’t worry, gaining skill and experience in your profession within these landmark projects will only help to propel your value for other EPC contractors further down the line.

What types of jobs do EPC Professionals do?

From painters, cooks, catering, welders, engineers, human resources, finance, law, project management, site representatives, drillers, surveyors, inspectors, IT, planners, logistics, controllers, doctors, nurses paramedics, firemen, health & safety, environmental…the list goes on and on. Basically, just about any profession you can think of can be found on these types of projects, in just about any part of the world! The opportunities are ENDLESS and so is your income potential! Discover YOUR potential!

Who are EPC Professionals?

EPC Professionals are regular people, just like you! We have the proven skills in our individual fields that companies are always looking for. Whether you’re fresh out of university, skill tradesman, seasoned professional or upper management EPC Professionals can assist you in finding your “dream” job!

Where are these jobs located?

Project can be located in any part of the world including but not limited to USA, Africa, Indian Sub-Continent, Asia, and Australia onshore & offshore. Whether in developing countries or major metropolitan cities; EPC Professionals are there!!

What kinds of salaries do EPC Professionals make?

Generally, you can make 2-3 times your normal salary as you would make in your home country. Understanding that your country of origin is the key!
